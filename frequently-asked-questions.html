Frequently Asked Questions

When will you launch?

We are hoping to launch our airline and commence operations within the next few years, however it is impossible to say currently.

What destinations will you fly to?

We are aiming to fly to numerous destinations, mainly regional Western Australia, some parts of Asia and some parts of other Australian states. You can find an ever changing list here.

What aircraft will you operate?

We will likely operate the Embraer 170 and the Airbus A320. More info is here including liveries

What type of airline is this?

We will be a generic passenger airline, however our Business class will be similar to First on other airlines, and our Economy will be similar to Business. We aim to offer these at prices close to other airlines

Are we looking for pilots, and other workers?

We are not currently looking for any workers, however you can contact us and we will let you know if we are!

Where will we be based out of?

We will be based out of Perth Airport (PER, YPPH)
